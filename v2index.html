Why We Exist

We started Garnet Cards with a simple belief: celebrating people shouldn't cost a fortune. Greeting cards have gotten unreasonably expensive, so we're bringing things back to basics with affordable, high-quality options.

Whether you want a physically crafted piece, a crisp studio print, or the ultimate convenience of a printable template you can use at home, we've got you covered.

Giving Back: We believe in supporting the community that supports us. We're currently planning how to best allocate a portion of our profits to causes that make a real difference.
